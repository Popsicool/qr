Location QR Code

Sharing your Google Maps location often makes it easier for clients to walk into your business location. Copy the link of your Google Maps Location and paste it into thr QR code generator to create Google Maps QR code.

Event QR Code

Make it easier for event attendees to add your event to their calendar, offer exclusive details, navigation and the chance to buy VIP passes, all with a single QR Code. You can use QR codes at the event to offer exclusive content.

Payment QR Code

Using this QR Code category allows users to send and receive money and make payments. The payment QR Codes can be used in a restaurant, retail stores, Movie theatres, events, small outlets, bars, cafes and many more.

Rating QR Code

Using a Rating QR code attracts new clients into your business. Use the QR code to direct customers to fill out a quick feedback form. You can place the Rating QR code on your flyers etc....

Call QR Code

You can use QR Code to make calls without having to dial the numbers manually. This Call QR Code can be placed on billboard, inside stores to help shoppers get in touch with salespersons.

Text QR Code

This can be used in educational institutes. It allows students to cross-check their answers. It also enables retailers to provide additional information about their products.
